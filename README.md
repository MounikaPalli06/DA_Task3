# Deep-Dive Analysis & Interactive Dashboarding using Power BI

## Project Overview

This project focuses on solving business problems through data analysis and interactive dashboarding. The objective is to analyze sales and customer data, define key business KPIs, perform a deep-dive customer segmentation analysis, and build an interactive Power BI dashboard for decision-making.

---

## Objectives

* Define and calculate core business KPIs.
* Perform deep-dive customer segmentation analysis.
* Generate business insights using data visualization.
* Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
* Present findings through visual reports and dashboards.

---

## Dataset

**Dataset Name:** E-Commerce Sales Dataset 2024

The dataset contains transactional sales records including:

| Column Name | Description                |
| ----------- | -------------------------- |
| order_id    | Unique order identifier    |
| customer_id | Unique customer identifier |
| order_date  | Date of purchase           |
| category    | Product category           |
| region      | Sales region               |
| revenue     | Revenue generated          |
| quantity    | Quantity purchased         |

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### 1. Total Revenue

Measures the total sales generated.

Formula:

Total Revenue = Sum(Revenue)

### 2. Total Orders

Measures the number of orders placed.

Formula:

Total Orders = Count(Order ID)

### 3. Total Customers

Measures the number of unique customers.

Formula:

Total Customers = Distinct Count(Customer ID)

### 4. Average Order Value (AOV)

Measures the average revenue per order.

Formula:

AOV = Total Revenue / Total Orders

### 5. Revenue per Customer

Measures average revenue generated by each customer.

Formula:

Revenue per Customer = Total Revenue / Total Customers

## Deep-Dive Analysis: Customer Segmentation

Customer segmentation was performed using K-Means Clustering based on:

* Total Revenue
* Total Quantity Purchased

Customers were grouped into:

* High-Value Customers
* Medium-Value Customers
* Low-Value Customers

This analysis helps businesses identify their most valuable customers and design targeted marketing strategies.

## Key Findings

* Electronics generated the highest revenue among all categories.
* The North region contributed the largest share of total sales.
* A small percentage of customers generated a significant portion of revenue.
* Customer segmentation identified high-value customer groups for targeted marketing.
* Revenue trends indicate steady business growth.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Focus retention efforts on high-value customers.
* Increase marketing investment in top-performing regions.
* Promote high-revenue product categories.
* Develop personalized campaigns for medium-value customers.
* Monitor customer purchasing behavior to improve customer lifetime value.
